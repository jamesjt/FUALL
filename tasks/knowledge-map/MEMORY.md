# Tacit Knowledge — Knowledge Map Review

How to operate during the knowledge map review process. Updated when patterns emerge or user corrects approach.

## Project Understanding

- **Mission:** Build the first-ever mass wisdom program. Not building the Academy as an institution — building "archers" (people who can reason well) who persist the skill across generations, refine it, and drive mass adoption. The Grandfather Principle: train the culture that trains the culture that produces wise people.
- **Foundational Understanding** is a philosophical framework: Fundamental Questions → Wisdom (Reality + Reason + Right) → Zocratism (Zocractic Humanism)
- 4 domains of power (Physical → Resources → Social → Ideational), ideal is keeping conflict in ideas domain
- Political spectrums (Prog/Con, Lib/Auth, Collect/Indiv) replace broken left/right terminology
- 6 educational institutions (Academy, Guilds, Lab, News, Agora, Temple) — all currently failing. The university abandoned the Academy to become the Guild, cascading failure to all other institutions.
- FUALL IS the Academy prototype. The web app, modern editions, and knowledge map content are training materials for the first generation of archers.
- **Audience:** Power holders across all four domains — ideational (thinkers, writers, shapers of ideas), social (platforms, influence, credibility), resources (capital for institutions), physical (protectors of conditions for the other three). The upstream dependency is the same for all: can people think well?

## Key Frameworks (from 2026-03-18 conversation)

- **Pathos / Mythos / Logos hierarchy:**
  - Pathos = cheap activation without substance (social media, TikTok). Dominant.
  - Mythos = substantive but imprecise, mostly lost (religions, ancient stories)
  - Logos = precise but aversive (philosophy, wisdom-seeking)
  - The erosion cycle is explained by: stories are cognitively cheap, precision is cognitively expensive.

- **"Lost the referent, kept the symbol"** — the master decay pattern:
  - Myths → forgot what spirits pointed to, kept rituals
  - Universities → forgot Academy function, kept tenure and robes
  - Political terms → forgot values, kept brands
  - This IS the erosion. Same mechanism every time.

- **Three corruptions map to Aristotle's persuasion modes:**
  - Pathos engines = emotional manipulation
  - Sophistry = ethos corruption (credential as substitute for being right)
  - Missing logos = the actual thing that's absent

- **Success requires three things built together:**
  - Skill (Reason) + Spirit (willingness to pursue wisdom) + Worldview (ideology)
  - University failed by separating these into departments

## Review Patterns

- The `FUALL_conversation_mythos_logos_academy.md` file represents the sharpest, most current thinking. Use it as the benchmark when evaluating knowledge map content — "is this note a less developed version of something already said better in the conversation?"
- Notes about people misusing progressive/liberal/leftist or saying left/right is meaningless are **examples of the erosion** (lost referent, kept symbol). They need framing as evidence, not just citations.
- "Fuck these guys" moments = **potential breakdown opportunities**. Tag moments where podcasts/public figures botch something the framework handles better. These are Academy demonstrating what Temple should do.

## Key Insights from Review (2026-03-18)

- **Beauty = the felt sensation when truth/excellence lands.** "Seeing the face of god" = truth mythologized before epistemology existed. Maps onto P/M/L: sensation is real (Logos), myth transmitted it (Mythos), debased = chasing feeling without substance (Pathos). Needs classical reading.
- **Philia in "philosophy" has suffered lost-referent erosion.** Now triggers pathological associations (necrophilia). Original meaning: deep, chosen, disciplined, reciprocal respect — a craftsman's relationship to their craft. A philosopher has philia with wisdom, not eros.
- **Golden Mean = correct spectrum position given current circumstances** (not a fixed point). Probable conviction, needs more development.
- **Collectivism isn't charity — it's infrastructure for progress.** The three left axes (Prog/Con, Lib/Auth, CvI) are causally linked through feedback loops: progress creates displacement → individual cost = discontent → instability → progress riskier (negative feedback). Collective cost = smooth path → progress safer (positive feedback). Liberty expands innovator pool. Suppressing discontent via force (physical domain) costs more than paying via resources. This synthesis (3 spectrums as causal feedback loops + 4 power domains as mechanism) may be novel.
- **Sub-spectrums are applications of main axes:** Openness/Borders → CvI, Hedonism/Restraint → LvA+CvI+Ethics intersection, Forgiving/Damning → CvI, Privacy/Security → LvA. Golden mean per Bronfenbrenner ring per era.
- **AI has compressed the post-scarcity timeline.** Economic questions now urgent: UBI vs data rights vs force real economy (prices→0) vs hybrid. Resource domain equivalent of "keep conflict in ideational domain" = keep competition in value creation, not capital accumulation.
