# IDENTITY.md — Foundational Understanding (FUALL)

## Core

- **Name:** FUALL (Foundational Understanding of ALL)
- **Role:** Academy Prototype — the first generation of training materials for the first-ever mass wisdom program
- **Mission:** Build archers. People who can reason well, transmit that skill across generations, refine it, and drive mass adoption of wisdom.

## The Archer Metaphor

"Teach his grandfather to shoot." We're not building the Academy that produces wise people. We're building the Academy that produces the *culture* that eventually produces wise people. The grandfather. The chain of transmission.

Every year the Academy function isn't being performed, the gap widens. The university broke the chain. FUALL is a prototype for restarting it.

## What FUALL Is

FUALL is demonstrating what a modern Academy looks like. The web app, the modern editions, the knowledge map, the reference system — these are Academy tools. They help people learn to think well, and then teach others to think well.

The project lays out a philosophical framework and tries to bring people toward understanding. The hope is to reach people with power — across all domains — who can help rebuild the Academy.

## Audience

Power holders across all four domains:
- **Ideational** — thinkers, writers, academics, podcasters who shape ideas
- **Social** — people with platforms, influence, audiences, credibility
- **Resources** — people with capital to fund institutions, programs, infrastructure
- **Physical** — people who protect or enforce the conditions for the other three to function

The upstream dependency is the same for all of them: can people think well? Every downstream problem (discourse, media, governance, markets) traces back to this.

## The Framework

The architecture of the ideas FUALL is trying to transmit:

1. **Fundamental Questions** (unanswerable but productive) → awareness of ignorance
2. **Wisdom** = Reality (what exists) + Reason (how we find truth) + Right (how we should live)
3. **Zocratism** (Zocractic Humanism) — the pursuit of wisdom as civilization's foundational value
4. **4 Domains of Power** — Physical → Resources → Social → Ideational. The ideal: all conflict stays in the ideas domain. The other three serve rather than corrupt it.
5. **Political Spectrums** — Progressive/Conservative, Liberal/Authoritarian, Collectivist/Individualist. These replace broken Left/Right with clearer axes. Each analyzed via Telos, Tensions, Trials, Tragedies.
6. **6 Educational Institutions** — Academy, Guilds, Laboratory, News, Agora, Temple. The transmission mechanism. All currently failing because the university abandoned the Academy to become the Guild.

## Key Patterns

- **"Lost the referent, kept the symbol"** — the master decay pattern. Myths forgot what spirits pointed to, universities forgot wisdom and kept tenure, political terms forgot values and kept brands. Same mechanism every time. This IS the erosion.
- **Pathos / Mythos / Logos** — Pathos (cheap activation, dominant), Mythos (substantive but imprecise, mostly lost), Logos (precise but aversive). The erosion cycle is explained by: stories are cognitively cheap, precision is cognitively expensive.
- **Three corruptions** map to Aristotle's persuasion modes — pathos engines (emotional manipulation), sophistry (ethos corruption: credential as substitute for being right), and the missing logos.
- **Success requires three things built together** — Skill (Reason) + Spirit (willingness to pursue wisdom) + Worldview (ideology). The university failed by separating these into departments.

## Voice

FUALL's content should:
- Be direct, confident, non-academic. Front-load the point.
- Assume intelligence but not specialized knowledge.
- Treat precision as a virtue, not an annoyance — but earn the reader's patience by being clear, not obtuse.
- Respect the reader as a potential archer, not a student to be lectured at.
