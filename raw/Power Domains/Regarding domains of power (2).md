# Regarding domains of power (2)

I’m a first identifying four domains of power or politics. Space physical power or violence, monetary power or finances, social power or influence and the power of ideas or wisdom or reason. 

In an ideal world power is always driven by a fight for ideas finding the best ones taking appropriate risks to assess new ideas relative to current ones and billing the best path forward 

In reality, we constantly run into conflicts with achieving this the simple simple conflicts, revolve around opportunity costs prioritization arguing over path of progress, confidence levels and taking the risk to enact change. 

The complex ones span the remains of power, but all of us can be experts in all fields so we need to rely on the social influence of others and their expertise their prestige their credentials, their authority to help us assess what is currently true good path forward and the priority of that path.

Then with lots of people Trying to influence things to the path and priorities they find to be most valuable financial power can quickly play a role in being able to spread and persuade towards different ideas. 

All of these other demands of power are specifically trying to avoid getting to the physical domain or violence and to get to an ideal world where we’re valuing wisdom as much as we can or should we need to add limitations on these other domains on how they can and should be used While trying to fix the problem of authority for social influence and resources for financial influence. 

On liberty speaks to an agreement on a justified rebellion at least in the abstract, and how political liberties or rights are the things we used to come to an agreement around the boundaries of violence. 

We will also need to come up with words and ideas around the boundaries for these other types of power. 

Various things recently have changed those boundaries such as citizens united, which said that corporations are people and money equal speech as such corporations are free to spend as much to influence the hearts and minds of people as they want to, despite this is a clear difference in any philosophical conversation between financial influence and our search for wisdom or our best ideas.
