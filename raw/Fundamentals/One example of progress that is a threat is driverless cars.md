# One example of progress that is a threat is driverless cars

The existence of this technology is a huge boon to society. It is change to all sorts of things in our job market etc. 

A significant percentage of people work in transportation, 2% of the population is truck drivers alone. 

if we could get driverless trucks huge aspects of our transportation infrastructure could be made safer, cheaper, and more reliable.
yet this change causes a 'security' issue for the truckers. Their livelihood is threatened by this, which can cause a lack of stability for society more generally. 

This could be resolved by us collectively paying for their security for the collective gain in the improvement we will all feel by this progress while 'buying' the societal stability in doing so.
