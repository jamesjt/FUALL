# How to find Truth

How to find Truth:

• "For the saying is 'Truth is the daughter of time', not '... the daughter of authority'!" - Novum Organum, Book 1 - Aphorism 84, Francis Bacon

• "veritas filia temporis" ("truth is the daughter of time") - Attic Nights, Book 12, Chapter 11, Section 7,  Aulus Gellius
    - Aulus Gellius attributed the phrase to an unknown "old poet," indicating it was already in circulation.

Not all change is improvement, but all improvement is change - D.M. Berwick
