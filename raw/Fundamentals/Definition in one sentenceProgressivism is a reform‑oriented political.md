# Definition in one sentenceProgressivism is a reform‑oriented political

1. Definition in one sentenceProgressivism is a reform‑oriented political philosophy that treats social, economic, and moral advancement as both possible and desirable, and therefore endorses active efforts—often through democratic government, science, and civic activism—to move society toward greater equality, rationality, and well‑being. Wikipedia

2. Core principles (across variants)- Belief in progress: Human conditions are improvable; tradition is not sacrosanct if better evidence or ethics emerge.
- Confidence in reason & science: Data, expertise, and experimentation are tools for policy.
- Moral egalitarianism: Reduce hierarchies based on class, race, gender, or wealth; expand rights and inclusion.
- Positive‑state theory: Government should regulate markets, curb monopolies, and provide public goods when private actors under‑serve the common interest.
- Pragmatism over dogma: Willingness to revise means if outcomes fall short—a “learning orientation.” Encyclopedia Britannica
