# Troubles with social science

"No man ever steps in the same river twice, for it's not the same river and he's not the same man" - Heraclitus

Source
- Fragment B12 from Arius Didymus in Stobaeus: “On those who step into the same rivers, different and different waters flow.”- Fragment B49a from Heraclitus Homericus: “We both step and do not step into the same river, we both are and are not.”- Fragment B91 from Plutarch: “It is not possible to step into the same river twice.”
