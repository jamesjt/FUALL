# Conservative - A belief that promoting and preserving the status quo is

Conservative - A belief that promoting and preserving the status quo is necessary to safeguard hard-won welli-being and understanding. The more conservative the more unwilling to risk what we have for a chance at a better future, and are more skeptical or resistant of anything claiming to be better.
