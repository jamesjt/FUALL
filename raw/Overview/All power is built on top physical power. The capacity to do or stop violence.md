# All power is built on top physical power. The capacity to do or stop violence

All power is built on top physical power. The capacity to do or stop violence. It enables, protects, and can destroy our ideas, resources, and people. 

An each of these other domains have a great amount of conflict within and between them. Where power should lie, toward what ends, and who should be able to wield different aspects of it is largely what we mean by Politics.
