# Power, by which I mean Political power, comes in a variety of forms, and a lot

Power, by which I mean Political power, comes in a variety of forms, and a lot has been said about how to think about power but the model that I've found most useful is considering it as 4 domains that make up a system. Any ideology needs to account for the relationships between them, how they can or should feed into each other, changes to the system (progressive vs conservative), who should get which powers (liberty and authority), and what they are used to benefit or hinder (collectivism vs individualism).
