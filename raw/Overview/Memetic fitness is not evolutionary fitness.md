# Memetic fitness is not evolutionary fitness

It is dependent upon the other domains of power, where evolutionary fitness is the physical domain, memetic fitness is the ideational. 

The right ideas might not succeed in an environment because the other domains of power aren't aligned with it. 

Across environments we can see how this can work, the rise of the west being a good example relative to other regions in the world. 

On long enough time frames there is memetic fitness, but memes are much more transmissible and integrate able  than genes currently are, and this allows people to who were not from that culture to easily catch up. 

The memes with the most fitness don't necessarily win in a generation because they need all of the other domains aligned with them, and when they aren't we see the erosion I mention in the intro.
