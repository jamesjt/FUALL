# Progressive - A belief that improving our understanding Reality and Rightz is

Progressive - A belief that improving our understanding Reality and Rightz is both possible and desirable. The more progressive the more willing to pay the costs, risk, or sacrifice what we have for a chance at a better future, and the more open to anything that might mean better.
