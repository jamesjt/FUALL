# Unfortunately, we don't have answers to these questions. We are ignorant, but

Unfortunately, we don't have answers to these questions. We are ignorant, but awareness of that ignorance is not a bad place to start...

That awareness lets us start figuring out how to be less ignorant. That we have the capacity to bring the confidence in our belief close to what is true. We have the capacity to Reason.
