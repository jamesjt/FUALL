# Progressive vs Conservative

Progressive vs Conservative

This spectrum is also an aggregate, it aggregates issues and various aspects of them. 

One might be progressive on a specific issue while conservative on another. 

With how fractured and confused the conversation is today  aggregate is limited by prior agreement and context, and really should be spoken about on where someone wants to create progress with the default being conservative to maintain what our current way of doing things. 
As such it would behoove progressive statesman to find a effective label for what where they want progress. This quickly leads to a fracturing of the progressives. In this aggregate a progressive would be someone is broadly unhappy with the current way of things and a conservative is broadly happy. 

Context might give more detail but the center of this spectrum is nearly useless in the aggregate as it could mean a large variety of things. Very unhappy about how we are doing some issues and very happy about some others. 

Moderately happy about every issue, and any combination in between, when creating a progressive movement it should be obvious and clear what and how that person wants to create progress or it gets lost into the aggregate and if you have a movement that is called 'the progressives' it is both difficult to create awareness, discussion, and wisdom about specific issues. 

This causes aggregate of progressives to quickly fracture and potentially across multiple dimensions. If if two people identify the same problem, they might have different solutions, and different plans to enact them. 

Broad progressivism is hard because of how quickly and easily it can fracture and create disagreement, which makes it even harder for conservative though to trust that there is a valid opportunity for progress in the first place. Progressives not to justify the progress to other progressives not just conservatives.
