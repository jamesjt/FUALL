# How we talk about Political spectrums is insufficient. They terms associated

How we talk about Political spectrums is insufficient. They terms associated with them should be more closely tied to their respective values, not their parties, leaders, base, or brand. 

These are important and meaningful terms tied to important and meaningful concepts that strongly related to how we work toward the Right way to live. 

[1] Of note, my terminology about Right way to live, in the since of just, improved well-being, or ideal runs into being a homonym of the 'Political Right'. I'm hoping context will mostly clear that up, in times it might be ambiguous I'm going to use Rightz meaning right way to live, and Rightp meaning the political right.
