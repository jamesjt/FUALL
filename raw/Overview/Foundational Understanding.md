# Foundational Understanding

The foundations of civilization are eroding...again

Our current way of doing things, our institutions, that guide our norms and values are losing utility.

All of those are failing because of something even more foundational: The transmission of ideas and our tool for doing it, language.

We need to rediscover ourselves...again...

Much of what I have to say isn't new - some of it, I think, will be - but much of it might be new to you. 

I'm striving to rebuild Foundational Understanding
