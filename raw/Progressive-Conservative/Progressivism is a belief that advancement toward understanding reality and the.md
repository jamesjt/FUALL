# Progressivism is a belief that advancement toward understanding reality and the

Progressivism is a belief that advancement toward understanding reality and the right path toward improved well-being is both possible and desirable. Humanity should allocate effort and resources toward paying the costs and taking the risks of progress to create a virtuous cycle of knowledge and flourishing.
