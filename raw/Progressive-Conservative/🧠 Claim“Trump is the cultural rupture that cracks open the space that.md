# 🧠 Claim“Trump is the cultural rupture that cracks open the space that

🧠 Claim:“Trump is the cultural rupture that cracks open the space that postliberals now fill.”
🔍 Why I made this assertion:This claim rests on three converging observations:
- Empirical emergence: The rise of explicitly postliberal thinkers (e.g., Deneen, Vermeule, Ahmari) accelerated sharply post-2016, in direct response to Trump’s disruption of fusionist conservative norms.
- Intellectual acknowledgment: Many postliberal thinkers themselves point to Trump as a turning point—not because he had a coherent philosophy, but because he broke the old conservative consensus, making space for new right-wing theories.
- Academic and journalistic recognition: Scholars, journals, and political commentators have started noting a post-Trump realignment on the right, with postliberalism as a leading contender to fill the ideological vacuum.
