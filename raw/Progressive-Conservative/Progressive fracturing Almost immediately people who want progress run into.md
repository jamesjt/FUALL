# Progressive fracturing Almost immediately people who want progress run into

Progressive fracturing: Almost immediately people who want progress run into fracturing. 

Fracturing can happen due to disagreement on:
- Areas we need to progress
- Priorities or ranking of those areas
- Strategy or tactics for achieving the progress
- Who pays for the costs of progress / Stability (are these the same things?)
- Memetic Corruption
- Efficacy at at different timescales
- Efficacy at specific system configurations
