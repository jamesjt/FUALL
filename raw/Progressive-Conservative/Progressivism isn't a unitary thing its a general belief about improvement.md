# Progressivism isn't a unitary thing its a general belief about improvement

Progressivism isn't a unitary thing its a general belief about improvement. part of its trails is dealing with its fracturing. It fractures across multiple dimensions. 
• Goals and alignment
• Opportunity costs, ranking order
• Strategy toward achieving those goals
• Displacement costs
• Dependencies and synergies
• Dangers of regress
