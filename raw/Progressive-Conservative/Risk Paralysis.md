# Risk Paralysis

Bar is to high
Rosy Glasses
Bad copying - Memetic mutation
 - Memes, like genes, fail to replicate perfectly, and many of our memes need to be very precise to maintain their utility. We see them get mutated on transmission on the time. Terms are not perfect representations of the concepts, and not all relationships of concepts are easily communicated even if terms were perfect representations. So miscommunication is common, especially over time. We're on a many multi-generation endeavor to get us to achieve wisdom (understand reality, reason, and right), so we need to transmit these ideas many times over, and many times that mutation has moved out societal trajectory into very bad places. Conservatism is generally attached to an authority, be it a Church, Tradition or person or otherwise. When that authority becomes mutated away from the correct path conservatism is the wrong force to correct it, as it's job is more focused on stopping outside forces from getting into the ideology rather than making sure the Memes it is trying to preserve are not mutating. This looks similar to the body immune system being blind to cancer.  

There seems to be a negative correlation for Memes that communicate easily and those that communicate deep and nuanced ideas with complex relationships. So the easily communicable memes are less likely to be fully understood, creating this cancer. 

As such the thing that is trying to preserve our provide ideas can and all to often does end up protecting a cancerous version of the idea. The more core the non-cancerous version of the idea is the more Conservatives will want to preserve it, forcing a more extreme progressive action to root out the cancer. 

Additionally because we don't have something like a UBI, and maybe even if we did, the people whose job it is to Conserve will find themselves in a situation where their personal well-being is threatened by rooting out and irradiating the cancer, they are effectively the cancer itself. 

  - Solved by: Building more effective transmission nuanced and complex memes. Create easily digestible versions of ideas will giving convient access to the deeper meaning. I.e. the Racket Timelines. Focusing on substantive clear ideas and then giving tools to trace them back and understand what they relate to
