# 🧭 DEFINITIONSTermShort DefinitionLiberal ConservatismA tradition that fuses

🧭 DEFINITIONSTermShort DefinitionLiberal ConservatismA tradition that fuses classical liberalism (individual rights, rule of law, market economy) with Burkean conservatism (social order, moral tradition, incrementalism). It seeks to conserve the liberal order itself.Postliberal ConservatismA newer movement that argues liberalism has failed, hollowed out community and virtue, and must be replaced with a more substantive vision of the good—often grounded in religion, moral realism, or nationalism.
