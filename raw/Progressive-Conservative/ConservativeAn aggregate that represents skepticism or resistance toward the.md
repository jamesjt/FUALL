# ConservativeAn aggregate that represents skepticism or resistance toward the

ConservativeAn aggregate that represents skepticism or resistance toward the risks involved in change, the accuracy of the claims those changes will result in improvements, and a belief that maintaining whatw we have is less important than sacrificing current well-being by allocating more resources to exploring or in-acting claims of progress. 

It is analogous to an immune system for society. Its purpose is to maintain the health of society, ensuring a stability by preserving core values, structures, and institutions. While properly vetting any proposed changes to make sure they don't cause harm.
