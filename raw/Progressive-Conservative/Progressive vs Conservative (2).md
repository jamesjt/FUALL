# Progressive vs Conservative (2)

Progressive vs Conservative
This quote is the tension of the spectrumNot all change is improvement, but all improvement is change - D.M. Berwick

This spectrum is actually a complex and layered aggregate, at its core, it reflects a belief about how Right our current way of doing things is, and what changes should be made to create improvement.

A person, movement, or party might be progressive on a specific issue while conservative on another. 

It contains not just a variety of issues but multiple dimensions per issue:
• Where do we see gaps, inefficiencies, injustices, or opportunities that call for change?
 - We commonly call these 'political issues'
• What is the strategy for enacting change?
• How to pay the costs of the change?
• Who implements the strategy?
• When is the right time to tackle specific issues, how to do prioritize changes?
