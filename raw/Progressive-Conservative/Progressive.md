# Progressive

Is also an aggregate that represents willingness to allocate resources toward, sacrifice a part of what we have, or risk some stability for a change toward chance at a better future, and the more open to anything that might mean better

It is a belief that advancement toward better understanding reality and the right path toward improved well-being is both possible and desirable. Humanity should allocate effort and resources toward taking the risks and paying the costs of progress to advance knowledge and flourishing.
