# At its heart, conservatism is in part analogous to an immune system for

At its heart, conservatism is in part analogous to an immune system for society. Preserving core ideology and structures to maintain a stable society and removing outside things that threaten harm, while also properly vetting and integrating new ideas to add on to and improve what is working.
