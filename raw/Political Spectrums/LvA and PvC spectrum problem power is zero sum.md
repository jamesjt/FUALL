# LvA and PvC spectrum problem power is zero sum

LvA and PvC spectrum problem: power is zero sum?
One of the problems with this spectrum or maybe that runs across all three is that power is fairly zero sum. 
What progress we make, how, and for who is largely tied to power. What future those with power are trying to create. 

While resources can become less scarce, unless we get to a existence where everything is post scarcity and no ones choices affect another persons in a meaningful way (no large scale problems like global warming, how to influence the crowd toward a specific ideology etc). There will always be opportunity costs, disagreements on vision of the better world, and costs of progress. 

Power is what will determine the paths for all of those things.
