# Authoriy vs Liberity is in part the balance of wisdom vs corruption

We have no mass wisdom program and never have, but the general idea is that to few people (as a percent of the population) have been wise enough to rule toward the 'right'. 

And the fewer the people the easier it is to corrupt them, as in have them work for themselves instead of for the collective. 

The idea of a democracy is dependent upon getting the floor of wisdom in a society to a sufficient level that society can best guide itself toward the right while minizing corruption. 

this is in part teh relationship between the Liberty vs Authority spectrum and the Collective vs Individualist one.
