# The agora is the place to discuss the lived experience where the everyday

The agora is the place to discuss the lived experience where the everyday people can come and talk about what's working, what's not. All of their anecdotes in life about what isn't good enough, what changes are working in which aren't. It won't be as rigorous as the laboratory environment in drying to ascertain efficacy of ideas of progress, but it will be faster and more agile.  This lived experience is meaningful in the same way that rigorous science is meaningful. for trying to assess the truth of things, the relationship between the two is still something I believe we're exploring. Where do we put weight on each at different times and because of the nature of the social sciences being very slow and difficult to find truth with, lived experience might be much more valued when science is as hard as it is to do in the social sciences.
