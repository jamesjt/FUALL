# If a man will begin in certainties he shall end in doubts, but if he will be

If a man will begin in certainties he shall end in doubts, but if he will be content to begin in doubts he shall end in certainties - Francis Bacon The Advancement of Learning (1605), Book I, chapter V, § 8
