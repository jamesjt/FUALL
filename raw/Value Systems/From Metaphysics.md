# From Metaphysics

From Metaphysics: 

It is right also that philosophy should be called knowledge of the truth. For the end of
theoretical knowledge is truth, while that of practical knowledge is action (for even if
they consider how things are, practical men do not study the eternal, but what is
relative and in the present).
