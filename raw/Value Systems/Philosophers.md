# Philosophers

By a philosopher’s, we mean, lovers of wisdom and by wisdom to a large degree what we mean is truth. 

What we should mean by philosopher is people that are good at getting to truth. 

Not people that have studied other people’s ideas. 

Truth is elusive in a whole bunch of ways running experiments is one way in which we can get better at getting to truth. Some experiments can cleanly get you there but most experiments require clear thinking an analysis post experiment to understand what happened to get to a less fuzzy picture of what is true. 

Good experiment design is another part of this process. 

Many experiments can’t be run well health education, a lot of things related to social science sciences 

We don’t have a clean laboratories for these things we can’t easily tweeze out individual variables. We can’t run the experiments again on the same subjects. 

So good social sciences requires really good philosophy because you are unable to do good experiments

You can just do better experiments than other people more than we did before. 

This is for a bunch of reasons the time scale, but also how adaptive the subjects are 

This might also prove to be true a bit in biology as we’re learning about the adaptability of biological development based off of RNA and Epigenetics
