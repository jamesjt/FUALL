# Ability to recognize the current landscape of things and adjust knowledge and

- Ability to recognize the current landscape of things and adjust knowledge and confidence levels to the current situation 
- Ability to project into the future the changing landscape to accurately predict a course of action to a specific result
- Ability to consider complex multivariable situations to create accurate advice. Often presented in abstractions to the problem leaving the details to others to fill in for their specific situation as it is generally time restrictive to get sufficient details. So getting to the important details to provide sufficient advice. 

All of this generally means it is mostly relevant to life advice. Like is always changing, requires prediction, and we don’t have time to dig into all of the details or often can’t even recognize all of the details in our own lives.
