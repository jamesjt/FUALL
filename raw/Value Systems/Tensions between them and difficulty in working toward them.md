# Tensions between them and difficulty in working toward them

First we don't live in paradise now. We don't have the perfectly ethical existence. It is part of what we're building and discovering. 

We don't even know enough about ourselves or what we should be to precisely say what that is yet. 

Besides our ignorance, resources, and time are our constraints. 

Time is a constraint not just because it is a resource, but because we are mortal and this is a many many generation project, so we need to solve for bringing up the next generation to share the same values, cultivate their wisdom, and their craft. 

Lot of time, insufficient resources, trade offs/opportunity costs, complex relationships (need to map that a bit). 

Balancing all of this ends up being one of the hardest problems, governance. 

Building and maintaining a culture that values these things and can balance each of their needs over time. 

The sorting problem, where we are now, liberty (capitalism and democracy)
