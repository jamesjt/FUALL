# So...how do we come to know

So...how do we come to know?
  Answer: Reason and then the scientific method.

I assume the student already has at least passing familiar with science. However, most people college educated and beyond seem to have very little understanding of what reason actually is. 

A incomplete but simple definition of reason is simply logic. By logic we mean the process of understanding the relationship between ideas and facts, and sometimes finding new facts or even inventions from those relationships. 

It might be worth mentioning that the scientific method is somewhat living. It has developed over time and may continue to develop.

This introduction course would spin off into a course more formally on reason and on the philosophy of science or generally epistemology.
