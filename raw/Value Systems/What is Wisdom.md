# What is Wisdom

What is Wisdom:
- Aristotle (metaphyscis):  Clearly then Wisdom is knowledge about certain principles and causes...the wise man knows all things, as far as possible, although he has not each of them in detail; secondly, he can learn things that are not easy for man to know (sense-perception is common to all, and therefore easy and no mark of Wisdom). He who is more exact and more capable of teaching the causes is wiser, in every branch of knowledge...for the wise man must not be ordered but must order, he must not obey another, but the less wise must obey him.
- Accurate confidence in one's beliefs
- Understanding the relationship of things, the ability to enact reason consistently and reliably.- Another aspect of reason is bringing order to chaos, something that aristotle touches on as wisdom, 'they must not be ordered but most order'. the ability to navigate and bring order to chaos. Comfortable and familar and to some degree at home in chaos
